# Videos Folder

Place your video files here (MP4, WebM, etc.)

## Important: Update Video List

After adding your videos, update the video list in `js/video-loader.js`:

```javascript
this.allVideos = [
    'videos/video1.mp4',
    'videos/video2.mp4',
    'videos/video3.mp4',
    // Add your video filenames here
];
```

Make sure the filenames match exactly!
